How dare you? | CL☁︎UDS CL☁︎UDS How dare you? 17 Apr, 2024 Visited a coffee shop the other day. Read a good book while listening to soft music. It was one of those places where people go to sit down and have a quiet moment. So relaxing. Then a phone starts ringing on the table across from me. Four people sitting there and no one answers. Ring, ring, ring… Even though my earbuds have noise cancellation, that never-ending ringing tone cuts right through. I got more and more annoyed, thinking to myself: “How dare you? No concern for other guests, who have paid to sit down and have a quiet moment. So disrespectful!” Then one of them notices the lit-up display. He turns to the owner of the phone and informs him about the situation – with sign language. Turns out it was a group of deaf people, and one of them had accidentally unmuted the phone. His facial expression explained to the whole room how sorry he was about the accident.
